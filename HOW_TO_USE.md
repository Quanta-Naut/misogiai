# 🚀 How to Use LaunchPad Platform

## 🎉 Congratulations! You've Created Your Startup!

Now let's explore all the features of your LaunchPad platform.

## 📍 **How to Access Pitch Rooms**

### **Method 1: Navigation Bar**
1. **Look at the top navigation bar**
2. **Click on "Pitch Rooms"** 
3. **You'll see live pitch sessions**

### **Method 2: Dashboard**
1. **Go to Dashboard** (click LaunchPad logo or Dashboard in nav)
2. **Look for "Quick Actions" section**
3. **Click "Join Pitch Room"**

### **Method 3: Direct URL**
- **Navigate to**: `http://localhost:3000/pitch-rooms`
